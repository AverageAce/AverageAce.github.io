In the Black (Impeller Studios) (2015-2017)

Impeller Studios was my first big break after finishing college. I owe a lot to Jack Mamais, the studio's Lead Designer and one of my professors at SCAD, for trusting me and seeing my ability to deliver on the game's demanding visual requirements. I got to wear a lot of hats in my time here as you will see from the ecclectic collection of renders and screenshots below. I was primarily a hard-surface artist, but I also acted as a texture artist, level designer, environment artist, concept designer, previsualization artist, tester, and many other roles. During my time with the team (2015-2017) we brought the game through Alpha and showcased the game at PAX East 2017. I miss this team greatly and wish them the best as they get closer to release.

Tools used: 3DS Max, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Substance Designer, Marmoset Toolbag
