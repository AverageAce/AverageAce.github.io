"Lone Wolf"" (2014)

This was one of my final solo projects during my time at SCAD. I am a massive Halo nerd (see SRS99D) and always dreamed of modelling my own Spartan. Looking back, there is A LOT I would change, but character art is not my specialty and for that reason I was pretty happy with how this turned out. I modelled the M739 SAW from Halo 4 along with it which ended up as a finalist in the Entelechy Awards that year. To know where you're headed, you have to know where you've been, and this piece definitely shows me how far my skills have come over the years.

Tools used: 3DS Max, ZBrush, Substance Painter, XNormal, Quixel SUITE, Marmoset Toolbag
