THAAD Skills Trainer (2017-2022)

Torch Technologies

This is a small selection of the work I did on the Terminal High-Altitude Air Defense (THAAD) Skills Trainer (TST) during my time at Torch Technologies (2017-2022). Most of my work has not been publically released as of 2024, but a link to the publically released video tour of the skills trainer is included below. The TST represented an effort to bring military training simulators in line with modern video game technology and polish, something our team delivered on. The TST was very well received by US servicemembers and created a paradigm shift in how military training is delivered. The TST shipped on Alienware devices paired with wide-screen monitors and was also available on the Oculus Rift and Microsoft HoloLens. I am very privileged to have worked on this team from its inception and I'm very proud of what our team accomplished in my time there.

Tools used: 3DS Max, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Substance Designer, Marmoset Toolbag
