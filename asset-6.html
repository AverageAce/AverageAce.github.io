Mech Concept (ca. 2014)

This piece was based off a beautiful concept by artist Chang Woo Lee. I recreated his work, with his permission, for a project in my junior year of college (2014). Unfortunately, most shots of this piece have been lost and this is the only one that remains. I would love to revisit this piece. At the time, it really pushed my limits being a piece that demanded mastery of both hard-surface skills and organic forms. It was certainly a challenge at the time but my professor pushed me to explore my limits, and I am grateful that he did. Thanks again to Chang Woo Lee. The original work can be found here: METAL BLACK [2009]

Tools used: 3DS Max, ZBrush, Marmoset Toolbag
