Game developer. Computer tinkerer. Soldier. Forging my own path since 2007.

Welcome! I’m Casey Arterburn, and I’m glad you’re here. My career has been anything but ordinary—driven by an insatiable curiosity and a knack for reinventing myself. It all began with a dream of creating AAA video games, which I pursued relentlessly, even when resources were scarce. After earning a BFA in Interactive Design and Game Development from SCAD in 2015, I worked on titles like In the Black and groundbreaking projects such as the THAAD Skills Trainer, blending my passion for immersive visuals with innovative problem-solving.

In 2019, inspired by my family’s military service, I enlisted in the Alabama Army National Guard as an IT Specialist. This pivot sparked a love for computers, cybersecurity, and the intricate dance of technology. My journey has since taken me from 3D artistry to IT leadership and into cybersecurity, where I thrive on tackling challenges and safeguarding systems.

When I’m not immersed in work, you’ll find me chasing down new hobbies, practicing guitar, learning French, diving into computer science, or losing myself in a great book. Whether it’s mastering code, chords, or conjugations, I’m always up for the next adventure.

Let’s connect and see what we can create together.
