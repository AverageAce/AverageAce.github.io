Pegasus Cockpit

After my work on the Hyperion, I began to make a name for myself in the realm of ship production. My art director at the time tasked me with creating the interior of an already existing ship: the Pegasus. This ship was commanded by a faction called Lethe and had no external viewports. The outside world was recreated on the inside of a sphere, the strongest shape in nature (did I mention the game is based on real physics?). This task was much more conceptual in nature than the Hyperion, and I was given much more creative liberty in the production of this piece.

Tools used: 3DS Max, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Substance Designer, Marmoset Toolbag, VRay, Unreal Engine 4
