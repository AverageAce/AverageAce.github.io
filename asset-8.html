Deep Siege (2015)

Deep Siege was the product of a senior project that I had the privilege of working on my last year at SCAD. I joined the project after its inception but quickly integrated with a team of incredibly talented concept artists, modellers, animators, character artists, sound designers, and material artists to deliver a high-quality experience. I was responsible for modelling and texturing hard-surface assets as well as some set-dressing. I am still so proud of this project and wish we could have polished it even further as I have no doubt it would have been a success. The game went on to win best environment art at the Entelechy Awards and place 2nd overall in the Autodesk CG Student Awards.

Tools used: 3DS Max, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Substance Designer, Unreal Engine 4, Marmoset Toolbag
