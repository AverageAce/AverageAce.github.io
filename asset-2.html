SRS99D Sniper Rifle (Halo) (2020)

This was a personal project I produced in 2022 as a pure passion project. Those who know me know that I am a massive Halo nerd. The sniper rifle is a classic and well-known Halo weapon and this version of it, from Halo: Reach, just has an awesome design. This project was a self-imposed hard-surface challenge and to this day I am in love with how it turned out.

Tools used: 3DS Max, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Marmoset Toolbag
